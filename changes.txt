Instead of Singers, lets do Decorations. A Player may choose to Decorate any or all Pots that have seeds in them at a Location. All players can see how many Pilgrims have decorated each Pot at a location.

Remove the concept of Cherished pots completely.

Increase the number of Pots in each Location to a value from 6 to 9.

Pilgrims have a number of energy points. The Maximum energy stored is based on a number of things:

New Pilgrim: 3 energy max
Pilgrim > 1 day old: +1 energy max
Pilgrim > 1 week old: +1 energy max
Visited all Locations: +1 energy max

These energy acheivements are shown on the Pilgrim record.

Each Location has a Nursery which is a list of Seed types. This includes all seed types of all plants in Pots at this location, the locations origin seed, plus all carried seed types of Pilgrims currently at this location.

Players may change which seed is in a Pot (Planting action), for one energy. They then cannot do another action for TENDING_DURATION ticks, which should be 1 second. Planting does not remove the carried seed.

If a player has no energy then they cannot do a Planting Action. Decorating and Travelling does not require

Players may swap their carried seed for any seed currently available.

Each Pot has a timestamp of when it was last planted in. If the time since planting is more than SEEDLING_TICKS, then the image changes from seed to a seedling version of the plant. There are more groth stages that happen at GROWN_TICKS,  FRUITING_TICKS and DEAD_TICKS. We need png graphics for each plant for each growth stage, and descriptions for each plant with each growth stage.

If the user Hovers a visited location, then show the symbol for which Origin seed can be found there on top of the locations point. If the location has not been visited, show a question mark icon.

The Here now widget should not say the ID of the pilgrims. Instead it should show each other pilgrim as a meeple shape. Use different colors for each meeple based on the state of the pilgrim.

There is a new concept called Vision. Each Pilgrim has a Vision. A Vision is made up of Rules. The Pilgrims Vision and Rules are shown in the Pilgrim Record.

A Rule might be "Have one Mirewort next to a Goldthread" or "Three Thornwhisper in total". Rules can include empty spaces. When a Pilgrim is created, its Vision has only two slots for Rules. Vision and Rules are shown in the Pilgrim Record. Here each rule has a button which lets you delete it, and its space gets replaced with a countdown when you will receive a new random rule to replace it. New Rules take RULE_REFRESH_TIME to arrive. Lets make this value 60 second to start with.

Rules are also shown in a widget underneath the Pot display in the Location tab. Here they show highlighted if that Rule has been met at the current Location.

Each Rule based on its difficulty (we should be able to set this manually in code to do balance passes) requires you to have the rule met at a number of different Locations. Between 1 and 3 locations. In the Pilgrim Record display of Vision and Rules, we show how many visited locations currently have this rule correct.

If the player has 1 rule completed at the required amount of locations, then they gain another Rule slot in their vision (requires RULE_REFRESH_TIME to be filled). If they have 2 rules complete, then they get another slot. Each time they complete another rule, they get another slot. 

For each rule that a Pilgrim has complete, they gain +1 energy max, and 2% movement speed bonus.
